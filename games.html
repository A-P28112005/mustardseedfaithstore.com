How to Play

  • Two players take turns marking X or O in a 3x3 grid.
  • The first to get three in a row (horizontally, vertically, or diagonally) wins.
  • If all cells are filled and no one wins, it's a draw.
  • Choose Rock, Paper, or Scissors.
  • Rock beats Scissors, Scissors beats Paper, Paper beats Rock.
  • Try to outguess the computer!
  • Guess a number between 1 and 100.
  • You have 10 tries to guess the correct number.
  • Hints will tell you if your guess is too high or too low.
  • Flip two cards at a time to find matching pairs.
  • Match all pairs with the fewest moves possible.
  • Challenge your memory and beat your best score!
