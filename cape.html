Cape Town: The Mother City

A world-class destination where mountains meet the sea, cultures blend, and every day is an adventure. Discover why Cape Town is one of the most beautiful and vibrant cities on earth!

Start Exploring

Table Mountain

Take the cableway or hike to the top for breathtaking views of Cape Town and the Atlantic Ocean.

Table Mountain Info

Robben Island

The prison that held Nelson Mandela, now a UNESCO World Heritage Site. Tours depart from the V&A Waterfront.

Robben Island Tours

V&A Waterfront

Shopping, dining, and entertainment by the harbor, with views of Table Mountain and access to Robben Island ferries.

Explore the Waterfront

Bo-Kaap

Iconic colorful houses, cobbled streets, and Cape Malay culture. A must-see for photographers and foodies.

Learn More

Kirstenbosch Gardens

One of the world's greatest botanical gardens, nestled at the foot of Table Mountain. Famous for its tree canopy walkway.

Visit Kirstenbosch

Boulders Beach

Famous for its colony of African penguins and unique granite boulders. Great for families and wildlife lovers.

See the Penguins

Cape Point

Dramatic cliffs, a historic lighthouse, and the meeting of two oceans at the tip of the Cape Peninsula.

Cape Point Info

Stellenbosch Winelands

Tour Stellenbosch, Franschhoek, and Constantia for world-class wines and beautiful vineyard landscapes.

Explore the Winelands

Travel Tips

  • Best time to visit: October to April for sunny weather and outdoor fun.
  • Getting around: Rent a car or use MyCiTi buses for easy access to attractions.
  • Stay safe: Keep valuables secure and be aware of your surroundings.
  • Don't miss: Sunset at Signal Hill, penguins at Boulders Beach, and the V&A Waterfront.
